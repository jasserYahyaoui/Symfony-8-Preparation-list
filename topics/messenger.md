## Symfony 8.0 notes
- **Top-level topic**: Since Symfony 8.0, Messenger is now a standalone top-level topic in the exam.
- **Increased importance**: The Symfony 8 exam has increased the portion and importance of Messenger-related questions.
- **Strategy reminder**: Focus on core concepts (messages, handlers, buses, middleware, worker lifecycle) and standard behaviors. Do not spend time on the configuration of third-party transports.
