## Release management and roadmap schedule - Guided Challenge

> **Phase:** Phase 2 (For Revision)


**Goal:** From memory, answer: What is the support duration for a standard release? For an LTS release? Which version is the LTS for Symfony 7?

<details><summary>Click to reveal Solution</summary>

- **Standard release**: 8 months bug fixes + 14 months security fixes
- **LTS release**: 36 months bug fixes + 48 months security fixes
- **Symfony 7 LTS**: Symfony 7.4 (the last minor of the 7.x branch)

</details>
