## License - Guided Challenge

> **Phase:** Phase 2 (For Revision)


**Goal:** Without looking, answer: What license does Symfony use? Can you use it commercially? Must you open-source your code?

**Hints:**
- It's a very permissive license.
- Think: MIT vs GPL vs Apache.

**Testing:** Run `composer show symfony/framework-bundle | grep license` to verify.

<details><summary>Click to reveal Solution</summary>

Symfony uses the **MIT license**. Yes, you can use it commercially. No, you do NOT need to open-source your code. You must keep the copyright notice.

</details>
